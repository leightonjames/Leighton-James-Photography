About

Who's behind the camera?

My name is Leighton James and I am 23 years old. I reside in Bristol, South West England.

I've been taking photos ever since I was 14 and my passion for photography is still as strong as ever. I love travelling and have taken my camera with me to some amazing countries; America, Australia, Egypt, Germany, Italy, Kenya, Netherlands, Spain, & Tunisia.

When I’m not taking photos, I manage the IT department for an International Development firm known as Development Initiatives. I hope you enjoy browsing my website. If you have any questions, click here to contact me.

Many thanks,
Leighton
